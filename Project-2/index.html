On The Go Breakfast Cookies

By: Toby Long

Let's face it, no one wants to get up any earlier than they have to. With this simple recipe, you can have a delicous and healthy breakfast snack prepared and ready to go as you rush out the door in the morning. On The Go Breakfast Cookies are seriously really good.

Total Time: 0:45
Prep: 0:10
Cook: 0:12
Servings: 8
