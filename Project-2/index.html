On The Go Breakfast Cookies

By: Toby Long

Summary

This is a simple, 3-ingredient breakfast cookie recipe.

ITEMS

  • Large mixing bowl
  • Several small mixing bowls
  • Measuring cup
  • A VERY large fork

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 cup oats
  • 2 bananas
  • 1/2 cup your choice of
    • Almonds
    • Coconut flakes
    • Raisins
    • Peanut Butter
    • Chocolate chips
    • Walnuts
